Services

Residential

From tidying up to deep cleaning, we work hard to make sure your home is spotless when we step out your door. Find the best options for your needs below:

Standard Cleaning: This type of service is best suited for clients that are on a weekly or bi-weekly cleaning schedule. It includes vaccuming, mopping, wipping down cabinets, appliances, floors, tidying up the rooms, kitchen and bathroom.

Deep-Cleaning: This is a great option for clients that are looking for a monthly or one time cleaning. It includes all the services above plus, cleaning inside appliances, cabinets, windows, trims, deep scrubbing bathroom tiles, tub and kitchen.

Commercial

We are here for you and your place of business! Day cares, offices spaces & more. Find the best options for your needs below:

Standard Cleaning: This service is best suited for small commercial spaces. We recommend scheduling weekly or bi-weekly cleanings. This service includes vaccuming offices, common areas,cleaning restrooms and tidying up.
